Schedule: We will have two lectures per week, on Tuesdays from 5 p.m. to 6 p.m. and on Wednesdays from 3 p.m. to 4 p.m., starting on June 15th, in room MW154.

Some references: There are many references listed on the notes. The ones below besides the main lecture notes are the ones I particularly like and could find legally available either on arXiv or on the author's webpages.

  • Lecture notes written by me specifically for this course.
  • Cannas da Silva, A.; Symplectic Geometry - overview written for the Handbook of Differential Geometry, vol. 2, 2004.
  • Meinrenken, E.; Symplectic Geometry, Lecture Notes, University of Toronto, 2000.
  • Holm, D. D.; Geometric Mechanics - Part I: Dynamics and Symmetry, 2nd edition, Imperial College Press, 2011.

Observation: If you find any mistakes on the main lecture notes, please let me know. If you're watching the videos at any moment and see that I made a mistake on the spot, also let me know.
